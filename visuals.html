N 370-242u

(2024) Audio-visual made for Kinomural 2024, it was screened as a part of an open call. I did both audio and video for it. Video made in Touch Designer, based on animated models of spheres with holes, with stylized shaders, and then heavily displaced. Variations of takes were rendered and edited. Video was prepared first, audio was based on it. For sound I used Behringer Neutron and Arturia Pigments. Beginning is based on old-school synth scifi sounds which then transition into more melodic and detuned part.

Distorted Perceptions

(2023) Piece made during monthly challange organized by Mesh label. Theme of the challenge was 'distorted perceptions'. On this one I tried Blender's geometry nodes and as usuall added a bit of detuned synth soundtrack.

Wub-wub

(2022) In collaboration with Maciek Sozański, we implemented audio reactive visuals in Unity (game engine), which were used at the 'Wub-wub' show in local club Uczulenie. We did 11 different scenes which evolved by themeselves and reacted to the music in different ways. Visual was displayed on the projector reacting to the music played live by DJs.
