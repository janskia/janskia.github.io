DMSO+PU

(2022) A visual made by Marek Hajduk. I did sound design for edited video. Sounds are based on behringer neutron and atruria pigments and edited carefully to match what's happening on the screen. Was screened at Kinomural 2022 in Wrocław as a part of an open call.

Rozjazd

(2024) Short film made in Kinofaktura, I did fooley, sound design and sound editing. All sound was added in post production, there were no sound from the film set. There were some more creative sound editing involved as theme of the film is that the main character hears sounds in his room with a delay.

Ciałem

(2024) Film made in Kinofaktura. I wrote and recorded music for it and did part of sound design. Music is mostly based on mellotron sounds, drones made out of pitched down trumpet, beringer neutron and a little bit of soft synths. Also one of Gzik's tracks is featured there. Caution, It's a horror film, watch at your own risk.
